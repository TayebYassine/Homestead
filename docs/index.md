# Welcome to Homestead

**Homestead** is a lightweight, open-source land claiming plugin designed to give players full control over their land. Perfect for survival and SMP servers, Homestead lets players claim chunks in any world while enforcing customizable claim limits.

Each claimed region includes 50+ customizable flags, enabling owners to configure gameplay settings, from PvP and mob spawning to block interaction and environmental effects. Whether you're building a town, managing a private base, or running a faction server, Homestead provides the tools for a secure and customized experience.

## Why Choose Homestead?

**1. Lightweight and Efficient**  
Homestead is built with performance in mind. The built-in caching system ensures that region protection checks are fast, even on servers with thousands of claims.

**2. Fully Customizable**  
Every aspect of Homestead can be modified to your server's needs. From custom language files to configurable flag defaults, you have complete control.

**3. Open Source and Free**  
Homestead is completely free and open source. You'll never need to pay for updates or essential features.

**4. Active Development**  
Regular updates bring new features, bug fixes, and compatibility with the latest Minecraft versions.

**5. Easy Migration**  
Switching from GriefPrevention, ClaimChunk, LandLord4, Lands, or HuskClaims? Homestead includes built-in migration tools to transfer your existing claims.
